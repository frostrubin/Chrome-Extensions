This text contains german Umlauts.

It was typed on an american (EN-US) keyboard layout.

Thanks to UmlautHelper, it was easy to get german umlauts into the text.

"Frühstück ohne Möhren, macht nicht glücklich."
"Ähnliches gilt übrigens auch für Brötchen, hörte ich."

This is achieved, by 
1. Installing this extension
2. Switching to Keyboard Language "UmlautHelper" in the Chrome OS Settings
3. Rapidly-typing key combinations ae or oe or ue or sss
   Or their uppercase counterparts
